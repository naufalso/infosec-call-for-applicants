MS/Ph.D. Scholarship Opportunity

Information Security and AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things) Lab
at Pusan National University - South Korea invites Indonesian applicants to student programs.

Benefits

Selected candidates will be supported with research scholarships
(1,800~2,100 USD/month for Ph.D. students and 1,400 USD/month for MS students).
The scholarship would cover both tuition fees and living expenses.


Research Interests

(include, but not limited to)


AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things)
• XAI (Explainable AI)
• AI Security and Privacy Issues (Adversarial Machine Learning, Federated Learning, etc)
• Theoretical Aspects of AI (Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, GAN, etc.)
• AI Applications to Smart Factory Smart City, and Financial Applications


Qualifications

• Has Indonesian nationality
• Has graduated or will be graduated by December 2022 (either Bachelor/Master)
• Has undergraduate GPA more than 3.25/4.0
• Has excellent programming skills
• Has good analytical thinking skills
• Familiarity with machine learning frameworks (e.g. Tensorflow or PyTorch)
• Has good English Proficiency (Preferably TOEFL > 500 or IELTS > 5.5 or TOEIC > 800)
• Has ability to present and report in English Team-oriented and target-oriented person
